About the Project

helloworld is a small passion project of mine that is meant to teach people the very basics of any language they can think of. The idea is to give the briefest of introductions to a language, with simple terms like 'hello', 'thank you', and so on, along with some fun facts.

My motivation for the project came from an excitement around language learning, as well as a love for traveling. I thought the project would be a nice combination of the two. It was also the perfect opportunity to further my skills in web development, a field I have always been curious about.

Thank you so much for checking it out! I hope you found it entertaining and/or useful.
